# TryHackMe

This folder contains my personal notes and progress while completing learning paths and rooms on **TryHackMe**.

The purpose of this repository is to document my cybersecurity learning journey, reinforce concepts through written summaries, and maintain a record of the practical labs I have completed. Rather than simply marking rooms as complete, I document what I learned, the challenges I encountered, and how I solved them.

## What You'll Find

* Learning path progress
* Room-by-room documentation
* Hands-on lab summaries
* Challenges faced and solutions
* Notes on tools, techniques, and cybersecurity concepts

## Documentation Format

Most room summaries follow a consistent structure:

* Learning summary
* Challenges Faced
* How I Overcame Them

This approach helps me review concepts efficiently and track my growth over time.
