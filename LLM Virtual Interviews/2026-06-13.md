## Areas for Improvement

### Interview Skills

- Answer the question directly before giving background information.
- Use specific examples instead of general statements.
- Keep answers concise and structured.
- Avoid underselling achievements.
- Focus on evidence and results rather than claims.

### English Communication

- Reduce long, run-on sentences.
- Eliminate unnecessary filler phrases.
- Avoid repeating the same idea multiple times.
- Improve answer structure and directness.
- Speak with clearer beginnings and endings to responses.

## Most Important Observation

The biggest bottleneck is **not cybersecurity knowledge**.

The biggest bottleneck is converting experiences into concise, professional, evidence-based answers.

Many answers contained good experiences but were weakened by:

1. Excessive background information.
2. Indirect answers.
3. Lack of structure.
4. Repetition.
