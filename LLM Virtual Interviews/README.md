# LLM Virtual Interviews

This folder documents my cybersecurity mock interviews conducted with the help of Large Language Models (LLMs). It serves as a personal interview journal where I record my performance, feedback, and progress over time.

The goal is to improve my technical knowledge, communication skills, and confidence while preparing for cybersecurity internships and job interviews.

## What You'll Find

* Weekly mock interview sessions
* Technical and behavioral interview questions
* Performance scores
* Strengths and weaknesses identified
* Areas for improvement
* Progress tracking across multiple interviews

## Purpose

This folder helps me:

* Measure my interview readiness over time.
* Identify recurring knowledge gaps.
* Improve my ability to explain technical concepts clearly.
* Track progress through consistent practice and feedback.
* Prepare for real-world cybersecurity interviews.

## Documentation Format

Each interview record typically includes:

* Interview Date
* Topics Covered
* Overall Performance Score
* Strengths
* Areas for Improvement
* Feedback & Reflection
* Action Plan for the Next Interview

## Goal

The objective is continuous improvement. By reviewing previous interviews, addressing weak areas, and measuring progress over time, I aim to become more confident and interview-ready for cybersecurity roles.
